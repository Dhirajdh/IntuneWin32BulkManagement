# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to a positive, welcoming, inclusive, safe, and productive environment for all.

## Our Standards

Examples of **acceptable behavior** include:
- Striving to be inclusive and respectful to everyone.
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being mindful and attentive to the impact of your words and actions.
- Offering constructive feedback.
- Accepting constructive criticism.
- Demonstrating empathy toward other community members.

Examples of **unacceptable behavior** include:
- Harassment, intimidation, and bullying of any type.
- Discrimination, prejudice, or hateful language towards a protected group.
- Threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sharing personal or medical information about yourself or others.
- Any form of plagiarism.

## Enforcement

The project maintainers will need to investigate any claim of violation. If the victim or a supporter reasonably believes a violation occurred, the corresponding maintainer will examine all evidence to determine whether a violation happened. If a violation is confirmed, the maintainers may take action ranging from a warning to a temporary or permanent ban from the community.
